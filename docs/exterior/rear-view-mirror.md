# Digital rear-view mirror redirect

Even with the pass-through window, it's a bit tough to see through your rear view mirror with the Tune M1 truck camper.

My 2024 Tacoma had the stock digital rear view mirror, so **[I bought the GG Offroad Digital Rearview Camera Relocation Kit](https://www.ggoffroad.com/store/p/digital-rearview-camera-relocation-kit-tundra-22-24-tacoma-24-psdz8)** and redirected my camera to the back top of the Tune M1!
