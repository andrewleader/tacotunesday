Ever tried cooking on a slanted stove? Not great. We built a simple leveling stove platform out of ABS plastic and a handful of M5 hardware so we can dial in a perfectly level surface no matter where we park.

It took a bit of hunting to find parts that all fit together — the bracket slots, ball head joints, and clamping knobs all need to be just the right size for M5 thread — but once assembled it holds rock solid even with a full pot on it, and adjusting is as easy as loosening a few knobs.

Check out the full parts list and build steps in the [Leveling Stove Platform docs](/build/kitchen/leveling-stove-platform)!
